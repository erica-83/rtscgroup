How to Export Your Data

✌

From Strava

  1. Go to strava.com/settings/profile
  2. Click "Download or Delete Your Account"
  3. Request your archive
  4. Or: open any activity, click "..." then "Export GPX"
⛰

From AllTrails

  1. Open alltrails.com and go to your profile
  2. Open a completed activity
  3. Click the "..." menu
  4. Select "Export as GPX"
⏱

From Garmin Connect

  1. Go to connect.garmin.com
  2. Open an activity
  3. Click the gear icon (⚙)
  4. Select "Export to GPX"
